The Effect of Level-Ratio Paradigm on DPOAE Fine Structure

Under Dr. Maryam Naghibolhosseini, we presented on otoacoustical emissions at the Acoustical Society of America conference in December of 2020. As a Research Assistant in the Analysis of Voice and Hearing (AVAH) Laboratory, I focused specifically otoacoustic emissions. I utilized MATLAB for data manipulation and analysis to support research across 10 subjects both in-phase and out-of-phase frequency levels on a triad of paradigms.

Research included: cross-comparison of voice raters using Excel for system optimization, MATLAB computation to develop machine learning intelligence, TeX markdown language in manuscript development, glottal attack, offset, and obstruction vocal fold measures.

To read more about our research check out AVAH's website. or our virtual iPoster from the conference.
